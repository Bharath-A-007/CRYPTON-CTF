üîç About Steganography:

Steganography is the practice of concealing messages or files within other non-secret text or files.

Unlike cryptography, which conceals the content of a message, steganography conceals the very existence of the message.

Common techniques include hiding data in image pixels, audio files, or even whitespace in text documents.
